# 🐾 Meet Blobby — Your Server Pet

Our server has a pet! It's **one shared creature** that the whole server raises together. Everyone's care adds up, everyone shares the rewards, and if we all ignore it for way too long... it moves on and a new one hatches. No pressure. 😅

Here's everything you need to know.

---

## How to play

Just type a slash command in any channel — start typing `/` and the bot's commands pop up. Try `/status` first to meet the little guy.

| Command | What it does |
|---|---|
| `/status` | See the pet card — its name, level, hunger, happiness, our star pool, and collection progress |
| `/feed` | Feed it 🍎 — raises **hunger**, earns **+3 ⭐** for the server |
| `/pet` | Give it a pet 🫳 — raises **happiness**, earns **+2 ⭐** |
| `/wish` | Spend **15 ⭐** to pull a random new pet into our collection ✨ |
| `/collection` | See how much of the collection we've discovered |
| `/rename <name>` | Rename the pet (up to 32 characters) |
| `/checkin` | Daily check-in to build your personal streak 🔥 |
| *(just chatting)* | Talking in the server quietly earns the pet **XP** — no command needed |

---

## The two things to keep up

The pet has two needs that slowly drop over time:

- **🍎 Hunger** — top it up with `/feed`
- **😊 Happiness** — top it up with `/pet`

They decay *slowly* (think days, not hours), so you don't have to babysit it. A few people checking in now and then keeps it perfectly happy.

---

## Stars & the collection (the fun part)

Every `/feed` and `/pet` drops **stars** into one shared server pool. Once we've saved up **15 ⭐**, anyone can spend them with `/wish` to pull a **random pet** — a new species and color — into our shared **collection**.

- There are **30 pets to collect** total (6 species × 5 colors each).
- Some species are **rarer** than others — `frost` is the big chase.
- Pull a duplicate? You get **5 ⭐ refunded**, so a wish is never wasted.
- The collection is **shared** — every wish anyone makes fills the same album.

Check `/collection` any time to see what we've found and what's still out there.

---

## Leveling up

The pet gains **XP** from everything: feeding, petting, wishing — and just from people **chatting in the server** (passive XP, limited to once a minute per person, so spamming won't help 😉). More XP → higher level. It's a team effort.

---

## Wait, can it... die?

Technically yes — but it's *very* hard to do by accident. The pet only passes away after roughly **15 straight days of total neglect** (nobody feeds or pets it at all). With any normal activity it'll live happily forever.

And if it ever does happen: a **brand-new pet hatches** as a fresh random species and color, and our **whole collection carries over** untouched. So nothing you've collected is ever lost.

---

## TL;DR

Type `/status` to say hi. `/feed` and `/pet` to care for it and earn ⭐. `/wish` to collect new pets. Just chatting helps it grow. Have fun! 🎉
